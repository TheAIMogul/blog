The Journey

From the Bronx to Brown University to Building AI Empires

Born in Russia, raised in the Bronx, emancipated at 13, adopted by Brown University alumni pastors—my story isn't typical, and neither is my approach. Now? I'm making AI accessible and profitable for everyone—from Fortune 500s to kids in Overtown.
